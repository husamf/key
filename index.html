RIGHTS IN ISLAM



Islam has prescribed that its followers give people their due rights. Parents, spouses, children, neighbors; all are given specifc rights according to their specifc roles in this world. Accordingly, mutual bonds and solidity can be established and strengthened between the individuals of a Muslim society.
